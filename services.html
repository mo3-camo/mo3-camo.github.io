Eye Exams

Emergency Eye Exam - Price varies

Experience peace of mind with our emergency eye exam service, providing immediate attention to urgent eye conditions such as eye injuries, severe pain, or sudden vision changes. Our skilled optometrists are equipped with the expertise and advanced tools necessary to quickly diagnose and treat emergent eye issues, ensuring prompt care and preserving your eye health.

Adult and Senior Eye Exams - 130$

Take charge of your eye health with our comprehensive adult and senior eye exam. Our experienced optometrists perform thorough assessments, including vision testing, eye health evaluations, and screenings for common eye conditions, providing personalized care to help you maintain optimal vision and overall eye wellness.

Children Eye Exams - 35$

Nurture your child's vision with our specialized children's eye exam. Our caring optometrists utilize child-friendly techniques to assess visual acuity, eye coordination, and detect potential issues early on, ensuring optimal eye development and setting the foundation for their academic success and overall well-being.

Contact Lens Exam - 165$

Discover the freedom of clear and comfortable vision with our contact lens exam. Our skilled optometrists provide thorough evaluations, including precise measurements of your eyes, to determine the perfect fit and type of contact lenses for your unique needs, ensuring exceptional comfort, visual clarity, and optimal eye health.

Eye Surgery

Cataract Surgery - Free consultation

Our cataract surgery consultation offers expert guidance and personalized care for individuals considering cataract removal. Our experienced optometrists will assess your cataracts, discuss surgical options, and provide comprehensive pre-operative evaluations to help you make informed decisions and achieve improved vision and quality of life.

Glaucoma Surgery - Free consultation

Our glaucoma surgery consultation provides specialized care and support for individuals exploring surgical treatment options for glaucoma. Our knowledgeable optometrists will evaluate your condition, discuss various surgical interventions, and guide you through the process to ensure the best possible outcomes in managing this sight-threatening condition.

Laser Eye Surgery - Free consultation

Experience the potential of laser surgery for vision correction through our laser surgery consultation. Our skilled optometrists will assess your eligibility, discuss available laser procedures such as LASIK or PRK, and provide personalized guidance to help you make an informed decision about achieving clear, spectacle-free vision.
